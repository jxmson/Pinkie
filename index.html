Welcome to the digital exhibit of artist Pinkie Thulo

Pinkie Thulo is a Maseru based graphic designer with a passion for art and design. She got her start at the Ladybird International Arts Academy where she completed a graphic design short course in July of 2022. Since then she has gone to exhibit at her work at The Black Experience in October of 2022 and again in December of 2022 where she received staggering reviews. She has worked in Adode Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop and has done packaging design to name a few. Her abstract and afro-centric style of art is one to a marvel at as it is indicatory of her African roots and the pride she carries for the African descent.
